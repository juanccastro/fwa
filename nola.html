01. Concept

The New Orleans Story Booth is an interactive art experience that uses artificial intelligence (AI) and film projections to collect and share the city’s best stories.

02. How it works

While the stories are being told, an AI (artificial intelligence) pick up key words and project images onto the back wall of the booth, while a second projector display colors and additional imagery onto the storyteller creating stunning visuals that complimented the personal naratives.
